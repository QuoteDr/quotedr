Getting Started

Log in, click "New Quote" from the dashboard, fill in your client's name and project details, then add rooms and line items. You can type items manually or use the AI voice feature to speak your quote out loud.

Go to Settings → Business Profile. Fill in your name, address, HST number, and tagline. This info appears automatically on every quote you send.
