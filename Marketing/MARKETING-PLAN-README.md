# QuoteDr Marketing Plan
*Created: May 2026 | Updated by Sonic*

---

## 🎯 Who We're Selling To

**Primary:** Solo renovation contractors, 1-5 person crews, Ontario/Canada first
**Secondary:** General contractors, painters, flooring guys, electricians — anyone who quotes jobs

**Their pain points:**
- Quoting takes hours on paper/Excel
- Clients ghost them on vague quotes
- No idea what a job actually cost when it's done
- Losing money on jobs they can't track

---

## 📣 Channel Strategy

### 1. Instagram (Strongest Asset Right Now)
700 warm followers on work account who already trust Adam. Pure gold.

**Content pillars — rotate these:**
- "Watch me quote a job in 10 minutes" — raw screen recordings, no polish needed
- "Contractor math" — before/after of quoted vs actual cost (Job Tracker makes this automatic)
- "Things clients always ask" — educational, positions as expert
- "Behind the scenes" — using QuoteDr on a real job, casual
- Pain point posts — "If you're still quoting on paper you're doing this to yourself 👇"

**Posting schedule:** 4-5x/week on @quotedr, 2-3x on work account cross-posting

---

### 2. Facebook Contractor Groups (Free, High Intent)
Dozens of active Canadian contractor groups. People already talking about business problems.

**The play:**
- Join 10-15 groups (Ontario Contractors, Canadian Renovation Pros, etc.)
- Spend 2 weeks being genuinely helpful — answer questions
- When quoting comes up: share the story naturally
- Never spam a link cold — earn it with context first

---

### 3. Home Depot Pro Desk
Contractors hang out there constantly. Underrated channel.
- Business cards: "Quote jobs in 10 min — quotedr.io — free trial"
- Strike up real conversations at lumber, tile, pro desk
- One contractor who loves it tells his whole crew

---

### 4. Reddit
- r/Contractor, r/Construction, r/HomeImprovement, r/PersonalFinance (job tracking angle)
- Be genuine first, mention QuoteDr only when it genuinely fits

---

### 5. Paid Instagram/Facebook Ads
**Don't start until 3+ organic videos are performing well.**
- Boost best performing post ($10-20/day to start)
- Target: men 25-50, trades/contractor/self-employed, Ontario → Canada
- Retarget website visitors first — cheapest conversions

## 💰 Pricing Recommendation
- **Basic tier:** $29/month, 14-day free trial, no free tier at launch
- **Pro tier:** $55/month, 14-day free trial, AI features, QuickBooks, floor plan scanner, smart import, and advanced quote tools
- **Early adopter deal:** First 10 customers can receive a locked-in launch price or bonus free months in exchange for honest feedback and, only if they want, a real video testimonial

## Job Tracker Positioning

Current launch-safe wording:
- QuoteDr supports Home Depot Pro account uploads/imports for job-cost review.
- Deeper Job Tracker features are coming after launch.
- Future goal: connect credit/debit card purchases, then prompt the contractor to assign new purchases to active jobs.

Avoid saying bank sync, card-linked prompts, or fully automatic job costing are live until those features are shipped.

## Blog Cadence

Start with one useful post per week. Weekly is enough to build SEO and give Adam content to repurpose into Instagram captions without making the blog feel abandoned. Each post should be written like a contractor explaining one practical thing to another contractor.
