Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, but they are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your data.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
