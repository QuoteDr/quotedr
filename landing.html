Why We Built This

I'm Adam — a solo renovation contractor in Ontario. For years, I quoted jobs out of Excel. Honestly? My spreadsheets looked great. Clients loved the detail. But a big quote could eat an entire day. Copy, paste, calculate, re-format, double-check, repeat. Staring at a screen when I could be on a job site.

I needed my time back. So I went looking for software. Sat through demos with the big names in the industry — slick products, sharp salespeople. But $300 a month? $800 a month as you grow? For features I'll never use? And basic stuff like client payment reminders locked behind a premium tier? No thanks.

So I built my own. QuoteDr started as a tool for me. Now it's for every contractor who's ever lost a Saturday to a spreadsheet, or sent a quote that looked like it was made in 1998.

Made by a renovation contractor. For the solo operator, the two-person crew, the small team that just wants their time back — and quotes that actually win jobs.

— Adam, Founder & Contractor
